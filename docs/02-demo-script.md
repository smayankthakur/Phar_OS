# 2-Minute Demo Script

## Click Path

1. Open `/client-demo`.
2. Click **Enter Demo**.
3. In `/demo`, click **Reset Dataset**.
4. Click **Run Competitor Drop**.
5. Open **Command Center** (`/`) and review recommendations.
6. Apply top recommendation.
7. Open SKU timeline (`/skus/<skuId>?tab=timeline`).
8. Open the audit entry.

## What To Say (Presenter Notes)

- “PharOS starts with live operational signals, not static reports.”
- “Rules convert those signals into actionable pricing recommendations.”
- “Guardrails prevent unsafe actions that could erode margin.”
- “Every applied action is auditable and visible in timeline.”
